French Toast

Description

French Toast is a timeless breakfast favorite, loved for its golden, crispy exterior and tender, custardy interior. Whether it's a leisurely weekend brunch or a quick weekday treat, this Perfect French Toast recipe will satisfy your morning cravings. It's easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings, making it a versatile and delicious breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of thick-cut bread
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter or cooking oil for frying
  • Maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh berries, or sliced bananas for serving

Instructions

  1. In a shallow dish or a wide bowl, whisk together the eggs, whole milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt until well combined.
  2. Preheat a griddle or large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add a bit of butter or cooking oil to prevent sticking.
  3. Take a slice of bread and dip it into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are well-coated. Allow any excess liquid to drip off.
  4. Place the dipped bread slice onto the preheated griddle or skillet. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and slightly crispy. Adjust the heat as needed to avoid burning.
  5. Serve your perfect French toast with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, a dusting of powdered sugar, fresh berries, or sliced bananas.
