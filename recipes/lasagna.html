Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, a perfect combination of rich, savory flavors and layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese. This classic homemade lasagna recipe will transport you to Italy with every bite. Whether you're preparing a special meal for family or friends or simply craving a cozy dinner for two, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that never disappoints. So, roll up your sleeves, and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 450g ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the white sauce

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other ingredients

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • Handful of finely chopped basil for garnish

Instructions

Prepare the meat sauce

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef until browned.
  2. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the chopped tomatoes.
  4. Add in the Italian seasoning, salt and pepper and let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes.

Make the white sauce

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan, egg, basil, parsley, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until smooth and creamy.

Assemble the lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a small amount of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  4. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce and a layer of shredded mozzarella.
  5. Repeat the layers, starting with noodles and ending with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Cover the dish with aluminium foil.

Bake and serve

  1. Bake the lasagna for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 30 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly
  3. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Garnish with finely chopped basil and serve!
